# about

About me

As a committed Public Safety Officer for the Town of Brookhaven,
my responsibilities encompass securing facilities, enforcing codes,
and collaborating with local agencies to maintain community
well-being. This role has honed my skills in public safety and law
enforcement, complementing my ongoing education in security
systems at Farmingdale State College.
Concurrently serving as an Intern at Siemens and a Constable
for Port Jefferson Village, I leverage my computer literacy and
networking knowledge from Cisco Networking Academy to
enhance operational efficiencies. My Eagle Scout honor and
healthcare provider certifications reflect my dedication to service
and preparedness, underpinning my goal to safeguard communities
through technology and security expertise.
